Sinigang Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Tamarind paste
  • Meat or seafood of choice
  • Various vegetables (eggplant, kangkong, string beans)
  • Fish sauce and other seasonings
Procedure:
  1. Boil tamarind paste with water to make the sour broth.
  2. Add meat or seafood, cook until tender.
  3. Add vegetables and seasonings.
  4. Simmer until vegetables are cooked.
Bulalo Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Beef shank with bone marrow
  • Corn on the cob
  • Greens (e.g., cabbage, pechay)
  • Salt and pepper
Procedure:
  1. Boil beef shank with water until marrow is soft.
  2. Add corn and cook until tender.
  3. Add greens and season with salt and pepper.
Adobo Rice Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Cooked rice
  • Adobo sauce (soy sauce, vinegar, garlic)
  • Chopped meat (chicken, pork, or beef)
Procedure:
  1. Stir-fry meat with garlic.
  2. Add adobo sauce and simmer.
  3. Mix with rice and cook until flavors blend.

Atchara Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Green papaya
  • Vinegar, sugar, and salt
  • Garlic, onions, and pepper
Procedure:
  1. Slice papaya into thin strips.
  2. Pickle with vinegar, sugar, and seasonings.
Lumpia Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Spring roll wrappers
  • Meat (ground pork, chicken)
  • Vegetables (carrots, cabbage)
Procedure:
  1. Mix meat and vegetables, season well.
  2. Wrap the filling in spring roll wrappers.
  3. Fry until golden brown.
