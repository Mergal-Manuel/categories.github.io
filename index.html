Sinigang Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Tamarind paste
  • Meat or seafood
  • Vegetables (eggplant, kangkong, etc.)
Procedure:
  1. Prepare a tamarind-based broth.
  2. Add meat or seafood and vegetables.
  3. Simmer until everything is tender.
Bulalo Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Beef shanks with bone marrow
  • Corn on the cob
  • Cabbage and pechay
Procedure:
  1. Boil beef shanks until tender.
  2. Add corn and simmer until soft.
  3. Mix in cabbage and pechay before serving.
Adobo Rice Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Cooked rice
  • Adobo sauce
  • Garlic
Procedure:
  1. Saut garlic in oil.
  2. Add adobo sauce and stir in rice.
  3. Mix until rice is evenly coated and heated.
Paella Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Saffron-infused rice
  • Seafood (shrimp, mussels, etc.)
  • Chicken or chorizo
Procedure:
  1. Saut chicken and seafood in a large pan.
  2. Add rice and saffron broth, then simmer.
  3. Cook until rice is tender and serve.
Atchara Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Grated green papaya
  • Vinegar and sugar
  • Carrots and bell peppers
Procedure:
  1. Mix grated papaya with vinegar and sugar.
  2. Add carrots, bell peppers, and spices.
  3. Let it marinate for several days before serving.
Lumpia Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Ground pork or beef
  • Carrots and cabbage
  • Lumpia wrappers
Procedure:
  1. Mix ground meat with vegetables and spices.
  2. Wrap in lumpia wrappers and seal edges.
  3. Fry until golden and crispy.
Ensaladang Talong Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Grilled eggplants
  • Tomatoes
  • Red onions
  • Vinegar and fish sauce
Procedure:
  1. Grill eggplants until skin is charred. Peel off the skin.
  2. Chop eggplants, tomatoes, and onions into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Mix with vinegar and fish sauce dressing before serving.
Tinola Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Chicken pieces
  • Green papaya
  • Ginger
  • Chili leaves
Procedure:
  1. Saute garlic, onion, and ginger in oil.
  2. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add water and simmer until chicken is tender.
  4. Add green papaya and cook until soft. Add chili leaves before serving.
Sizzling Sisig Fried Rice Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Cooked rice
  • Crispy sisig
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Eggs (optional)
Procedure:
  1. Saut garlic in a hot pan until fragrant.
  2. Add crispy sisig and stir for a few minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce and mix well.
  4. Stir in cooked rice and toss until evenly coated.
  5. Optional: Add beaten eggs and mix until cooked through.
  6. Serve sizzling hot.
